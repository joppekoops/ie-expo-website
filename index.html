Immersive Expo

Save the date: January 19th, 2024
16:00 till 20:00 at the NDSM Treehouse

Sponsors

Visit our Expo at NDSM Treehouse to immerse yourself in a world of digital-physical spaces.

Students of the Minor Immersive Environments at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences will present the immersive installations they designed and produced for all these special project partners:

  • Cinekid
  • Digital Life
  • Infinia/Marisonia
  • Mood
  • Commonplace

As well as their very own personal projects!

be prepared to be immersed by projection, sound, objects, scents, sensors, and interactive walls. Hope to see you there!
